Purpose

This project aims to create an interactive experience that allows users to visualise their breathing through direct interaction. In this process, users generate their own breathing tracks. These tracks are not pre-defined, but are gradually formed through interaction, allowing users to externalise a process that originally belongs inside the body into a visible form.

This interaction is intended to make breathing which is a process that is usually unconscious become visible and perceptible. At the same time, as users interact, they will gradually notice the slow passage of time and the subtle rhythms of the body. Therefore, this tool is not only a creative tool, but also a way of guiding attention.

Worth

In today’s digital society, people are constantly exposed to information overload. In such an environment, it is becoming increasingly difficult for people to stop and become aware of their internal state. As a result, psychological issues are becoming more common, and most people experience feelings of irritation and anxiety to some extent.

This project provides a peaceful space where users can temporarily step away from this state of information overload. Instead of continuing to receive information, users interact with their own breathing and create something that belongs to and represents themselves. By constructing their own breathing track, users may better understand their emotional state. This tool does not deliver content but invites users to feel themselves. It also allows users to relax through repeated breathing and feel a moment that belongs only to them.

In this sense, its value lies in creating a space for introspection, where users can find a quiet state and reconnect with their own feelings.
